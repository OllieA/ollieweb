The Coliseum in Rome

We have booked passage on what looks like a fantastic cruise: Rome to Istanbul in October, 2012.

The cruise line is Regent Seven Seas who we sailed with on an Alaska cruise several years ago. We were very impressed by the accommodations and service, so when we saw this cruise on a sister ship (the Seven Seas Voyager) we decided it was the way to celebrate our 45th anniversary.


Istanbul

The Seven Seas Voyager is a 670 ft. long, 700 passenger all-balcony vessel. We found its sister ship, the Mariner, to be wonderfully comfortable and luxurious on the Alaska cruise, so we are looking forward to this stay. When compared to the almost-1,000 foot, 2,000+ passenger ships that commonly sail, we expect we will get a small, almost clubby atmosphere. Lots of ship comparison here at NauticalCities. Also, Cruise Critic gives the ship (and Regent cruises) very high marks.

In this piece of news, the Seven Seas Voyager just completed a complete refurbishment in September before starting this year's Mediterranean season.
